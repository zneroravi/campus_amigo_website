About Us

a group of people filling the gap between the company`s demand and academic structure to increase the productivity of students.

There is a gap between companies and students in terms of the hiring process companies want to hire new people and increase their productivity while students want to get hired and work but due to the gap they both are not able to achieve their goals companies have to settle for a subpar employee while students remain underpaid or even unemployed. We as a group of people feel they this gap should be filled.

“Campus Amigo” is a team which will prepare the students for the tough fight towards employment while molding them into worthy candidates who are interview ready. We will teach and will emphasize on production-ready tools and paradigms while improving your interview preparation and take your interview fear away.

What We Do

We Prepare the students for the tough fight towards employment while molding them into worthy candidates who are interview ready.

  • We will increase your current knowledge and make it interview ready for answering tricky questions.

  • We will teach production languages (java, python, node, etc) which are used in real life problems.

  • We will teach you how to clear the written round to move ahead with further rounds.


  • We will take mock test for written round and mock interviews for interview practice and to increase your confidence.

  • We will monitor you in all aspects through the entire process and will provide feedback according to your performance.

  • We will focus on your weak points and make you aware of the present scenario to improve further.

  • We will host competitive rounds to check your position and to increase your technical skills to help in interviews (future release).

  • We will provide you internships/jobs in highly paid companies (future release).
